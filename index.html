Method note

How the regional need signal is calculated

Score = 0.30C + 0.25S + 0.20D + 0.15P + 0.10I

C = connectivity gap; S = skills gap; D = institutional distance; P = service-demand pressure; I = rural population share. For this briefing view, C is operationalised as 100 - internet browsing rate, S as 100 - youth literacy rate, D as regional centroid distance from Windhoek normalised to 0-100, P as regional population normalised to 0-100, and I as rural population share. Census-based variables are drawn from the 2023 Population and Housing Census. Higher values indicate a stronger planning signal for capacity-building attention; they are not an official ranking.
