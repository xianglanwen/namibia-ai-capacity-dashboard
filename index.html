Method note

How the draft priority score is calculated

Score = 0.30C + 0.25S + 0.20D + 0.15P + 0.10I

C = 100 minus the 2023 Census internet-browsing rate. S = 100 minus the 2023 Census youth literacy rate. D = regional centroid distance from Windhoek, normalised to 0-100. P = 2023 Census regional population, normalised to 0-100. I = 2023 Census rural population share. High priority is 55 and above, medium priority is 40-54, and low priority is below 40.
