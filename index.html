Method note

How the regional need signal is calculated

Score = 0.35A + 0.20L + 0.20D + 0.15R + 0.10P

A = digital access gap, calculated as 100 - [(0.6 x internet-browsing rate) + (0.4 x smartphone ownership)]. L = 100 - the 2023 Census adult literacy rate for persons aged 15 years and above. D = regional centroid distance from Windhoek, normalised to 0-100. R = 2023 Census rural population share. P = 2023 Census regional population, normalised to 0-100. Higher values indicate a stronger planning signal for capacity-building attention; they are not an official ranking.
